When to Choose Each Path

Choose Aggressive Path If:

  • You have exceptionally strong investor interest (easy to raise)
  • You want to prove profitability ASAP before raising Series A
  • You're comfortable with hyper-focused product strategy (1 product only at start)
  • You have existing customer pipeline or distribution advantage

Choose Moderate Path If:

  • You want balanced growth and profitability (BEST FOR MOST STARTUPS)
  • You're raising from institutional investors who value sustainability
  • You want to build a strong multi-product foundation early
  • You prioritize ending Year 1 with significant profit + cash for optionality

Choose Conservative Path If:

  • You're bootstrapping or raising from angels/friends & family
  • You want to minimize dilution and preserve equity
  • You're testing product-market fit before aggressive scaling
  • You prioritize longest runway and lowest risk over speed
