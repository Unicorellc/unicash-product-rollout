Glossary of Terms

ARR (Annual Recurring Revenue)
Total annualized value of recurring revenue contracts. For UniCash, calculated as monthly revenue √ó 12.
ARPU (Average Revenue Per User)
Average monthly revenue generated per user across all products. UniCash target: $33.93/month by Year 3.
CAC (Customer Acquisition Cost)
Total marketing and sales expenses divided by number of new users acquired in that period. UniCash target: $28-35.
Churn Rate
Percentage of users who stop using the product in a given period. Target: <8% monthly.
EBITDA
Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization. A measure of operating profitability.
Gross Margin
Percentage of revenue remaining after subtracting cost of revenue (COGS). UniCash target: 58-60%.
LTV (Lifetime Value)
Total profit expected from a user over their entire lifetime. Calculated as: ARPU √ó Gross Margin √ó Average Customer Lifespan.
LTV/CAC Ratio
Lifetime value divided by customer acquisition cost. Healthy SaaS: >3x. UniCash target: 17-44x.
MAU (Monthly Active Users)
Number of unique users who engage with the product at least once per month. Target: 80-85% of total users.
MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue)
Predictable monthly revenue from subscriptions and recurring products (assistance plans, insurance).
MTL (Money Transmitter License)
State-level license required to transmit money, including remittances and cash advances. UniCash needs 50+ state licenses.
Net Margin
Net income as a percentage of revenue. Shows bottom-line profitability. UniCash target: 18% Year 1, 36% Year 3.
Payback Period
Number of months to recover CAC from gross profit. Formula: CAC √∑ (ARPU √ó Gross Margin). UniCash: 0.8-2.1 months.
Take Rate
Percentage of transaction value kept as revenue. Cash advances: 8.5%, Remittances: 2.8%.
TAM (Total Addressable Market)
Total revenue opportunity if UniCash achieved 100% market share. 62M underbanked Americans √ó average spend = $167B TAM.
