Emergency Cost Reduction Plan

If revenues fall below 50% of target in any quarter:

Immediate (Week 1)

  • Freeze all non-essential hiring
  • Cut marketing spend by 50%
  • Renegotiate vendor contracts
  • Reduce monthly burn by $200K

Short-term (Month 1)

  • Delay product launches (insurance, burial)
  • Reduce team to 12 people
  • Extend runway to 18+ months
  • Begin bridge round fundraising

Strategic (Quarter)

  • Pivot to best-performing products only
  • Explore strategic partnerships/M&A
  • Focus on profitability over growth
  • Secure credit facility for cash advances
